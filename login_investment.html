Applying for a MIGA Guarantee

The first step to apply for a MIGA guarantee is to file a Preliminary Application (PA). The free, confidential PA should take no more than 10 minutes to complete.

Once the PA is submitted and registered by MIGA, you will receive a Notice of Registration and an underwriter will contact you shortly thereafter to discuss your investment and determine its eligibility for a MIGA guarantee.

A step-by-step guide to MIGA's underwriting process is available here. For additional information, please email migainquiry@worldbank.org, call 1.202.458.2538, or visit www.miga.org/investment-guarantees.
